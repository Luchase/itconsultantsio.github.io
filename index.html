IT consultants and remote engineers network of Bangladesh

Who/what/why?

Remote work and freelancing has become a booming industry in Bangladesh. A lot of us are new to this type of work and do not have the knowledge required to navigate our professional careers successfully. With itconsultants.io, our goal is to share knowledge, provide help in placements, and build a community of remote working professionals.
